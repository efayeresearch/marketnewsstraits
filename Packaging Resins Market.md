<p data-start="397" data-end="1098"><strong><a href="https://straitsresearch.com/report/packaging-resins-market">Packaging resins</a></strong> are materials used in the manufacturing of packaging products to protect and preserve items during storage, transportation, and retail processes. These resins play a critical role in packaging materials such as bottles, containers, and films used across a variety of industries, including food and beverages, pharmaceuticals, consumer goods, and personal care. The use of resins like polyethylene (PE), polypropylene (PP), polyethylene terephthalate (PET), and polystyrene (PS) enhances product safety, extends shelf life, and contributes to sustainability efforts. Packaging resins are essential in meeting the growing demand for both functional and eco-friendly packaging solutions.</p>
