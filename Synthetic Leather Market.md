<h3 data-start="864" data-end="890"><strong data-start="868" data-end="890">Market Definition:</strong></h3>
<p data-start="892" data-end="1414">Synthetic leather, also known as artificial leather or vegan leather, is a material designed to replicate the properties and appearance of natural leather. Made from various polymers such as polyurethane (PU) and polyvinyl chloride (PVC), synthetic leather offers a cost-effective, durable, and cruelty-free alternative to animal-based leather. It is widely used in a variety of industries, including fashion, automotive, furniture, and consumer goods, due to its versatility, lightweight nature, and ease of maintenance.</p>
<p data-start="1416" data-end="1778">Synthetic leather is produced using advanced technologies, which provide it with an appearance and feel similar to genuine leather while being more environmentally friendly. The rising awareness of sustainable practices, along with the increased demand for vegan and cruelty-free alternatives, is significantly boosting the market for synthetic leather globally.</p>
