<p data-start="582" data-end="603"><strong data-start="582" data-end="603">Market Definition</strong></p>
<p data-start="605" data-end="1226">PET packaging refers to containers, bottles, and other packaging products made from polyethylene terephthalate (PET), a type of plastic. PET is widely used in packaging products for food, beverages, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals due to its ability to preserve the integrity of the product, resist moisture, and offer a lightweight and durable alternative to glass or metal containers. PET&rsquo;s popularity stems from its excellent barrier properties, cost-effectiveness, and recyclability. PET packaging also stands out in environmental sustainability, offering one of the highest rates of recycling among plastic materials.</p>
