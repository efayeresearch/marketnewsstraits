<p data-start="974" data-end="1091"><strong data-start="1097" data-end="1119">Market Definition:</strong></p>
<p data-start="1120" data-end="1635">Taste modulators are food ingredients that influence the perception of taste in food and beverages. They are used to modify the natural taste by enhancing or masking specific flavor profiles, such as sweetness, bitterness, or saltiness. These substances allow food manufacturers to create healthier products by reducing sugar, salt, or fat content without compromising on taste. Taste modulators are typically used in the production of functional foods, beverages, snacks, and low-calorie, clean-label food options.</p>
